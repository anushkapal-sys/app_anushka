About

The Smart City Tour Guide is an innovative system to enhance tourism using IoT, AI, and mobile apps. It delivers real-time information, personalized recommendations, navigation assistance, and cultural insights. By leveraging social media, travel blogs, images and geotags, the multimodal reranker improves relevance and context-awareness compared to popularity-only methods.
